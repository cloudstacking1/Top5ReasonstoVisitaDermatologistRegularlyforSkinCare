What is Dermatologist?

A dermatologist is a medical doctor who specializes in diagnosing, treating, and preventing skin, hair, and nail disorders. They are experts in managing a wide range of conditions, from common issues like acne, eczema, and psoriasis, to more serious concerns such as skin cancer and autoimmune skin diseases. Dermatologists use various methods, including medications, topical treatments, surgeries, and cosmetic procedures, to improve the health and appearance of their patients' skin. They are also trained to perform routine skin checks and offer advice on skincare to maintain healthy, glowing skin.
